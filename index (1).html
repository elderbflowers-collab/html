About Us

Pastor Beverly Flowers, M.Div.

Ordained Elder — United Methodist Church, South Georgia Annual Conference
Master of Divinity in Pastoral Care & Counseling, Gammon Theological Seminary
20 Years U.S. Air Force — Certified Master Addictions Counselor & Human Relations Instructor
Author: From the Basement to the Pulpit: Rescued, Restored, and Ready for More
Founder, The Oil of Joy Ministry Global Mission, Inc.

Pastor Beverly Flowers is a retired Ordained Elder in the United Methodist Church with over 24 years of ordained ministry and 20 years of distinguished service in the United States Air Force. She is the founder of The Oil of Joy Ministry, an Evangelical mission purposed to spread the Good News of Jesus Christ throughout the world.

Serving as an Associate Evangelist in the South Georgia Annual Conference, Pastor Beverly assists in pulpit ministry at Joycliff UMC in Macon, Georgia, delivering powerful sermons during the 11:00 AM service each fourth Sunday. She holds a Master of Divinity in Pastoral Care and Counseling from Gammon Theological Seminary, Interdenominational Theological Center, Atlanta, Georgia.

Pastor Beverly is available for speaking engagements, revivals, workshops, and funeral/eulogy services. She co-leads the Noon Day Bible Study at Kelley Chapel UMC alongside her husband, Rev. Dr. Joe Flowers Jr.

The Book

From the Basement to the Pulpit

In this powerful memoir, Pastor Beverly Flowers shares her remarkable journey — from humble beginnings to 24 years of ordained ministry and 20 years of military service. This is a testimony of God's rescuing grace, restoring power, and readying hand for every season of life.

Guided by her life's theme — "God's glory in my story" — Pastor Beverly invites readers into an honest, Spirit-filled account of how God transforms broken places into pulpits of praise.
