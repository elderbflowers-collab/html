About Us

Pastor Beverly Flowers, M.Div.

Ordained Elder — United Methodist Church, South Georgia Annual Conference
Master of Divinity in Pastoral Care & Counseling, Gammon Theological Seminary
20 Years U.S. Air Force — Certified Master Addictions Counselor & Human Relations Instructor
Author: From the Basement to the Pulpit: Rescued, Restored, and Ready for More
Founder, The Oil of Joy Ministry — in partnership with Joycliff United Methodist Church, Macon, GA

Pastor Beverly Flowers is a retired Ordained Elder in the United Methodist Church with over 24 years of ordained ministry and 20 years of distinguished service in the United States Air Force. She is the founder of The Oil of Joy Ministry, an Evangelical mission purposed to spread the Good News of Jesus Christ throughout the world.

Throughout her ministry, Pastor Beverly has been invited to preach at churches across the country, including Church of the Disciples UMC in DeSoto, Texas, Memorial Baptist Church in Kansas City, Missouri, Union Memorial UMC in St. Louis, Missouri, and First African Missionary Baptist on St. Simons Island, Georgia. She has served as Southeastern Jurisdictional Coordinator for the National Black Clergywomen of the United Methodist Church and has provided workshop trainings for the Pathways to Healthy Churches in the South Georgia Conference.

Today, alongside her husband The Rev. Dr. Joe Flowers, Jr., Pastor Beverly continues her calling — teaching, preaching, and serving with the same passion that has defined their ministry together for over two decades.

What We Do

Our Ministry

🕊️
Evangelizing Missions

Carrying the Good News of Jesus Christ beyond the walls of the church and into local communities and around the world. It is our mission to reach those who are hurting and introduce them to God's gift of salvation and the joy of the Lord.

🙏
Pulpit & Preaching Ministry

Pastor Beverly serves as a fill-in preacher and Associate Evangelist, delivering anointed messages each fourth Sunday at Joycliff UMC, Macon, GA, and accepts invitations to preach at churches.

📖
Bible Study & Teaching

Through Noonday Bible Study and special teaching engagements, Pastor Beverly equips believers with deep biblical understanding, connecting scripture to everyday life with clarity, wisdom, and pastoral heart.

✉️
Pastoral Care & Counseling

Drawing on her M.Div. in Pastoral Care and Counseling, Pastor Beverly provides compassionate support to the grieving, the hurting, and those navigating life's most difficult seasons — walking alongside families in love and prayer.

🌍
Our Mission

Inspired by Rev. John Wesley's declaration — "The World Is My Parish" — The Oil of Joy Ministry exists to share the love and compassion of Jesus Christ with all people, everywhere, without exception.

From being discovered as an abandoned 15-month-old toddler in a basement, to standing behind the pulpit as an Ordained Elder in the United Methodist Church — this is a story that only God could write.

Pastor Beverly's memoir traces her remarkable journey through military service, seminary training, and 24 years of pastoral ministry across Georgia and beyond. It is a story of abandonment turned to adoption, of struggle turned to strength, of the basement turned to the pulpit.
